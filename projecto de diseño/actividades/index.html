Roatan Divers is an award-winning dive operator based in West End. As a boutique operation, we cater to small groups and focus on personal attention, comfort, safety and environmental responsibility. We have our own docks for easy boarding, four custom designed dive boats and keep our groups and service very personal. With a fantastic team of knowledgeable instructors and experienced captains, combined with our great locations and some of the best scuba diving in the Caribbean at our doorstep, we are able to offer you first class scuba courses and dive excursions to an underwater world that will take your breath away!
